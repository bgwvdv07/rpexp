At Reppard's Landscaping, we transform outdoor spaces into sustainable, vibrant landscapes. Serving Oakland, Berkeley, and Alameda, we specialize in landscape design, garden installation, irrigation systems, fence & gate installation, and tree & shrub care.

Our mission is simple: to provide high-quality, eco-friendly landscaping services that enhance the beauty, value, and sustainability of every property we work on.
